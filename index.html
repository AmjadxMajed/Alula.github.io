About

DISCOVER A LAND OF LEGEND

An open-air museum the size of a country (22,561 km2), AIUla is certain to take your breath away. Located in northwest Saudi Arabia, on the original incense road and pilgrimage route to Makkah, this historical land is steeped in the immense beauty of the natural world, alongside the dramatic splendour of human creations. An ancient oasis in a desert valley, the site was a hub of trade and cultural exchange for millennia, and still bears witness to those who passed through. Golden sand stretches to the horizon, and rocky outcrops emerge from the desert's expanse. Thousands of tombs burial places can be found in AlUla, including more than 100 intricately carved tomb facades at Hegra, which stand majestically among mammoth sculptures carved by nature.
